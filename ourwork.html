Our Work

An International Center for Research and Innovation in Cyber Threat Resilience(CRICTR) will be launched during this workshop.CRICTR aims to do research, innovation, consultancy and training in the broad areas related to the development of cyber threat resilient information technology infrastructure capable of cyber threat prevention, detection and self healing. CRICTR will also provide a platform for providing Consultancy services to Government and Corporate entities in specific knowledge areas related to security. CRICTR research collaborations include participation from several national and international researchers and organizations.

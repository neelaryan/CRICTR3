About C.R.I.C.T.R

Given the highly publicized incidents of cyber penetration of anything from cars to airplanes in flight, as well as massive break-ins into systems amounting to industrial and strategic espionage, the idea of creating resilient and robust systems is attractive to government, businesses and individuals. Our customer research suggests that people are worried about three things: one the loss of sensitive data, two, the ability to recover from attacks with minimal damage, and three, the consequences of hostile penetration and capture of critical infrastructure.

In particular for India, as the country moves towards the age of Digital India, and more governance takes place via the cyber worlds, the threats to the systems are multiplying. In the current Indian context, it is imperative that we build cyber threat resilient ICT infrastructure for supporting the continued governance and growth of our economy. Research and innovation for the development of imparting cyber threat resilience as a core functionality of critical ICT infrastructure is also being explored internationally.
